Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Portfolio Builder?
A Portfolio Builder is an online tool that allows you to easily create a professional portfolio or resume. You can fill out a simple form with your details, and the tool will generate a beautifully designed portfolio in no time.
How does the Portfolio Builder work?
Simply enter your personal information, skills, experience, education, and other relevant details into the provided fields. The builder will automatically create a resume or portfolio based on your input.
Is the Portfolio Builder free to use?
Yes! You can create a portfolio for free with our basic template.
Can I customize the template?
Currently, the platform offers a single template for simplicity and uniformity, but we focus on providing you with customizable content sections like skills, experience, and about me.
How do I download my portfolio?
Once you’ve filled out your details, you can preview your portfolio and download it as an HTML File. Then you can host it on any hosting platform or simply share the HTML file.
Can I create multiple portfolios or resumes?
Yes, you can create as many portfolios as you want, using different versions or details for different job applications or opportunities.
